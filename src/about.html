About Us

Home Building Hub is an independent podcast designed to provide education and value to anyone looking to build a new home with a volume home builder in Australia. Hosted by industry experts Colin Bischof & Darren Brennan, the Home Building Hub is a conversational podcast that provides insights to help better educate listeners about new home building so they can avoid costly mistakes, but more so enjoy their building experience regardless of which home builder they choose.
